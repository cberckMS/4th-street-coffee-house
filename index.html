Our Story

4th Street Coffee House is more than just a coffee shop — it's a gathering place for the Stromsburg community. We believe in serving great coffee in a warm, welcoming space where neighbors become friends.

Every cup is brewed with care, and every visitor is treated like family. Whether you're stopping in for your morning coffee, catching up with friends, or just need a quiet spot to relax, we're glad you're here.
